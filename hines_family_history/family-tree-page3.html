Our family tree is the starting place for our family history. Here you can find details of your birth and some of the story of your childhood written from Mum’s point of view. Obviously, your recollections of events may differ significantly from me and it would be great to have you all add to your “story” including details of your spouses and children if applicable. Writing of special events in your lives will help create a living document which will be invaluable for your future generations. I encourage you all to add in photos, videos, documents and any other item that you feel is important to share.
